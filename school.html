Science Exhibition Winners

May 25, 2025

Congratulations to the winners of this year's Science Exhibition! Check out their amazing projects.

More...

The Science Exhibition 2025 featured an impressive array of innovative projects from our students. Winners were selected based on creativity, scientific accuracy, and presentation skills. Highlights included a working model of a solar-powered car and a detailed study on local biodiversity. Congratulations to all participants and a special shoutout to the winning teams from Class 7 and Class 9!

May 20, 2025

A Parent-Teacher Meeting will be held to discuss student progress and upcoming events.

Read More...

The Parent-Teacher Meeting scheduled for May 20, 2025, provided a valuable opportunity for parents to discuss their children's academic performance and behavior with teachers.
